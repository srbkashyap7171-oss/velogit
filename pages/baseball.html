Gentle Escapes

Step into comforting dimensions with these masterpieces of cozy fiction and gentle adventure.

1. A Psalm for the Wild-Built

Becky Chambers' hopeful solarpunk novella about a tea monk and a robot seeking purpose.

2. The House in the Cerulean Sea

TJ Klune's heartwarming story about a caseworker finding love and family in a magical orphanage.

3. Legends & Lattes

Travis Baldree's cozy fantasy about an orc who hangs up her sword to open a coffee shop.

4. Kiki's Delivery Service

Eiko Kadono's charming tale of a young witch finding her independence.

5. The Midnight Library

Matt Haig's novel about all the lives we could have lived, and finding peace with the one we have.
