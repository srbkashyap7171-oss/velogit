Biographies & Memoirs

Dive into the true stories of historical figures, innovators, and cultural icons.

1. The Diary of a Young Girl

Anne Frank's intimate and tragic account of life in hiding during the Nazi occupation of the Netherlands.

2. Steve Jobs

Walter Isaacson's riveting biography of the creative entrepreneur whose passion for perfection revolutionized six industries.

3. Becoming

Michelle Obama's deeply personal memoir about her roots and her time as First Lady of the United States.

4. Long Walk to Freedom

Nelson Mandela's moving autobiography detailing his early life, coming of age, and 27 years in prison.

5. Educated

Tara Westover's memoir about growing up in a survivalist family in Idaho and her quest for education.
