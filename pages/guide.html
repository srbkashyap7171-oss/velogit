Reading for Relaxation

Learn how to turn reading into a daily ritual for peace, disconnecting from screens, and calming your mind.

1. Create a Sanctuary

Designate a comfortable corner of your home for reading. Add soft lighting, a blanket, and remove digital distractions.

2. Choose Low-Stakes Fiction

  • Cozy Mysteries
  • Slice-of-Life Fantasy
  • Nostalgic Classics

3. Audiobooks for Sleep

  • Listen to calming narrators before bed.
  • Set a sleep timer to ensure you drift off peacefully.
  • Avoid intense thrillers at night.

4. Slow Reading

Don't rush. Savor the language and imagery. Reading is not a race; it's a retreat.
