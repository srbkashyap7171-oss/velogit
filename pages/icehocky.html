Calming Classics

Explore timeless works that have provided comfort and philosophical insight for generations.

1. Siddhartha

Hermann Hesse's spiritual journey of self-discovery and the search for enlightenment.

2. Walden

Henry David Thoreau's reflection on simple living in natural surroundings.

3. Meditations

Marcus Aurelius's personal writings on Stoic philosophy and maintaining inner peace.

4. The Alchemist

Paulo Coelho's fable about following your dreams and listening to your heart.

5. Tao Te Ching

Lao Tzu's fundamental text of Taoism, emphasizing harmony with the natural order.
