Mindfulness & Meditation

A selection of powerful guides to help you cultivate presence and find calm in a chaotic world.

1. The Power of Now

Eckhart Tolle's spiritual classic that teaches the importance of living in the present moment to find true peace.

2. 10% Happier

Dan Harris's skeptical and humorous journey into meditation, proving it's not just for yogis but for everyone.

3. Wherever You Go, There You Are

Jon Kabat-Zinn's accessible introduction to mindfulness meditation for everyday life.

4. The Miracle of Mindfulness

Thich Nhat Hanh's gentle letters offering practical exercises to bring awareness to daily tasks.

5. Think Like a Monk

Jay Shetty's guide to training your mind for peace and purpose every day.
